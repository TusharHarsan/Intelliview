About Deepfake Detection

Welcome to our Deepfake Detection project. We are dedicated to developing cutting-edge technology to identify and combat the spread of deepfake media.

Our team of experts uses advanced machine learning algorithms and artificial intelligence to analyze videos and images, detecting subtle inconsistencies that may indicate manipulation.

With the rise of deepfake technology, it's more important than ever to have reliable tools to verify the authenticity of digital content. Our mission is to provide these tools and contribute to a more trustworthy digital landscape.
